### Yummy Buddies 3D animated series

Join the Yummy Buddies in hard-to-access locations as 
they try to make sense of complex concepts.

On their adventures, they encounter challenges that require their quick wits, critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, and communication skills to overcome.

### Collaborate with us!
We are constantly working on new educational content for the Yummy Buddies.

Whether it’s a suggestion or an idea for the Yummy Buddies, let us know what’s on your mind! If you would like to be notified when there is new content, simply join our mailing list. (No spam. We promise!)
